# BA Lab Workbook Overview

This document explains the structure behind the BA Lab workbook.

## Core thinking model

Every meeting is analysed through 5 lenses:

1. Change
2. Risk
3. Stakeholders
4. Dependencies
5. Decision quality

## Workflow

### Before the meeting
- understand the context
- identify the real change
- prepare the right questions

### During the meeting
- challenge vague answers
- surface risks
- identify missing owners and dependencies

### After the meeting
- summarise what changed
- extract requirements and actions
- identify missed questions and gaps

## Goal

To help Business Analysts think more clearly and perform with more confidence in real discussions.
