# BA Lab Meeting Checklist

Use this checklist before and after every meeting.

## Before the meeting

- Do I clearly understand what is changing?
- Do I know the objective of the meeting?
- Have I identified potential risks?
- Do I know who needs to be involved?
- Do I understand what decision needs to be made?

## During the meeting

- Am I asking clear and specific questions?
- Am I challenging unclear answers?
- Have I identified risks and dependencies?
- Do I know who owns the decision?
- Am I capturing key points (not everything)?

## After the meeting

- What was decided?
- What is still unclear?
- What risks were identified?
- What actions need to happen next?
- Who is responsible for each action?

## Reminder

You are not just taking notes.

You are:
- clarifying problems
- identifying risks
- driving better decisions
