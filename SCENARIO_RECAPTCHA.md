# Scenario: reCAPTCHA Threshold and Customer Experience

## Problem

A high reCAPTCHA threshold risked blocking legitimate users during checkout.

## Why that mattered

This created a trade-off between:
- fraud prevention
- customer experience
- conversion and revenue

## BA thinking focus

- What is the business impact?
- What risk are we accepting?
- Who signs off the decision?
- Can we reduce friction while monitoring risk?

## Key trade-off

Security versus revenue and customer experience.

## Learning point

A strong BA helps stakeholders make the trade-off visible and drives clarity on ownership and decision-making.
