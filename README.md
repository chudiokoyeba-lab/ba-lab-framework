# BA Lab Framework

BA Lab is a practical framework designed to help Business Analysts perform better in real meetings.

## What it does

BA Lab helps analysts:
- prepare before meetings
- ask better questions during discussions
- identify risks, stakeholders, and dependencies
- turn messy notes into structured outputs afterwards

## Why it exists

Many Business Analysts understand theory, but struggle to apply it under pressure in real meetings. BA Lab is designed to close that gap.

## Who it is for

- Junior Business Analysts
- Career switchers
- Analysts building confidence in live discussions

## Current focus

This repository contains early public materials for the BA Lab framework, including:
- change discussion workbook concepts
- scenario walkthroughs
- practical questioning structures
- example outputs

## Example scenarios

- Payment flow and shell orders
- reCAPTCHA threshold trade-offs
- stakeholder and decision ownership prompts

## Status

Early public framework repository. Actively being refined through practical testing and real meeting support use cases.
